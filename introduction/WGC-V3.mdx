## What WGC V3 Enables

WGC V3 is designed to move freely between networks, enabling frictionless use across diverse ecosystems — from trading and integrations to liquidity provisioning.

It functions as a **cross-network currency layer**, combining accessibility with a scalable, future-proof architecture.

---

## Core Features

### **Fixed Supply**
The total supply is capped at **5 billion WGC**, minted at genesis on Ethereum.

---

### **Omnichain Architecture**
- Built using **LayerZero OFT** + **Superchain ERC-20**  
- Fully fungible across 25+ networks  
- Uses **native burn-and-mint**, not wrapped tokens  
- Unified supply across all chains  
- Transfers can be executed through interfaces like **Stargate** in a single step  
- Users typically pay only the **source chain gas fee**  

No slippage, no custodial risk, and no liquidity bridging assumptions.

---

### **Efficient Transactions**
On most supported chains:

- Finality in **under 1 second**  
- Fees typically **under $0.01**  

---

### **Scalable Deployment**
WGC’s architecture allows new blockchains to be added with minimal friction.

---

### **Permissionless Participation**
Anyone can:

- trade WGC  
- provide liquidity  
- integrate it into apps  
- expand the ecosystem  

No central approval or coordination is required.
